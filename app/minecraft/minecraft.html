 Version Upgrade

World Meld

The two same-seed worlds that were running on ports 25565 and 25566 have been merged into a single mega-world!

  • Inside of Mike's Castle: THE NEXUS has appeared!
       This room acts as a centralized hub for teleportation.
  • East of Maloo's House: Ganon's Castle has appeared!
       Mine carts rails are run between Mike's and Ganon's Castles.
  • Above Mike's Castle: Chris' Sky Island has appeared!
       Either THE NEXUS or the waterfalls on the castle grounds can take you up to the island.
New Content

Many new additions to enjoy.

  • The Far North: Imperial City has appeared!
       Teleporters are available to take players to and from the city.
  • West of Laura's Village: Hyrule Castle has appeared!
  • North of Laura's Village: Several new structures have appeared!
    • Pokemon Stadium Super Smash Brawl
    • Military Air Force Base
    • Nuclear Missile Base w/ Reactors
    • Spawn #2
    • Airship Shipyard - Royal Navy Shipyard at Emerald Bay
    • The MOAB (Mother of all Beacons)
    • Observation Tower
    • The Big Eye
    • Pyramid Adventure
    • Desert Island Towers
  • Atop Yalda's Castle: The Master Sword has appeared!
